Social Engineering

What's Social Engineering?

Social engineering in the context of information security is the psychological manipulation of people in order to carry out actions or disclose confidential information. This is different from social engineering in the social sciences, which is not related to the disclosure of confidential information. A type of trust trick for information collection, fraud, or system access purposes is different from a traditional trick in that it is usually one of many steps in a more complex fraud scheme. At the same time, social engineering is one of the easiest and least time-consuming Hacking methods. Attackers first collect enough information about the target, and then they can easily hack the target with the information they have.

How Is Social Engineering Done?

Of course, fake emails are at the beginning of Social Engineering techniques. After the attacker has determined his target, he sends a number of fake emails to his email to lure his target into the trap and waits for him to be trapped.

What's Setoolkit?

What'S Setoolkit? Setoolkit is the most well-known and widely used Social Engineering tool. There are many options for the tool. The website has a lot of options for cloning, fake emails, fake links and much more. Setoolkit is available inside the Kali Linux system. You can run the tool by typing ' setoolkit ' in the terminal and you will be faced with many options. Each option has a number that you can run by entering the number next to the option you want to select.
