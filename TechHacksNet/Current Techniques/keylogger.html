What's KeyLogger?

KeyLogger or Keystroke is one of the most dangerous information theft techniques known. In such attacks, the attacker, after determining his target, sends a number of e-mails to his target and tries to lure his target into a trap. In these e-mails, they usually store a link, they can store it in a word file with an image. In order to install KeyLogger on the target's system, the target must necessarily click on the link, except that in no way does the KeyLogger penetrate the target's system and the attacker does not get what he wants.

So What Does The KeyLogger Doing?

KeyLogger saves literally all the keys you clicked after you hacked into your system. Your credit card information, e-mail addresses, personal data can be seized almost all of your data.

Are KeyLogger Is A Virus?

Yes, KeyLogger is considered a virus, but unlike other viruses, it shows no symptoms on your system. But if it is not sufficiently embedded in the file, it can be detected and blocked by Antivirus programs.
