What's Hack?

In general terms, hacking means obtaining unauthorized access to the system. People who provide this access, that is, those who perform this action, are identified as "hackers". Although many hackers are against the recognition of hackers, the definition of hackers has started to be used all over the world due to the influence of the media.

What Programing Language We Need To Know For Hacking?

For beginners, you need to know Python, Php, Javascript and similar languages. for advanced hacking, you need to know the C language, Perl, Shell, Sql, Ruby languages at an advanced level.

What Can Be Hacked?

Every device that can be connected to the Internet can be accessed and hacked. As an example, Phones, Computers, Televisions, Smart Watches, Bluetooth Headphones, Fridges, Vacuum Cleaners, Elevators, Cars Connected to a Local Server, and many other Smart devices that can be connected to the Internet can be hacked. What's Linux?

- What is Linux or What is the Linux Operating System? Linux is an Open Source operating system and is the fifth most preferred operating system in the world. Linux, which has more than one version, also has many distributions. The most widely used distribution of Linux is Ubuntu, followed by Unix in the second place. But the distribution that we will consider is the Kali Linux distribution. Hacking or Penetration testing the most preferred Linux distribution is Kali Linux.

What's Kali Linux? What Does It Do?

- What is Kali Linux? What Does It Do? Kali Linux is a Linux distribution developed in 2013 for security checks. It is the simplest and Most useful operating system among Linux distributions, which is usually used for Decontamination tests. Kali Linux has a High user base because it is very well developed in terms of ease and understandability.
